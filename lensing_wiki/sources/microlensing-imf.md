# Sources: microlensing IMF

## Jiménez-Vicente & Mediavilla 2019 — lens-galaxy IMF

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Jimenez-Vicente2019`
**Reference:** ApJ 885, 75; arXiv:1910.10509; doi:10.3847/1538-4357/ab46b8
**Concepts:** [[microlensing-imf]]

**Supports:**
- Microlensing magnifications for 24 lensed quasars constrain the average stellar mass in the lens galaxies without fixing the IMF shape in advance.
- The inferred mean stellar mass is 0.16 solar masses, with quoted 68% confidence bounds of +0.05 and -0.08 solar masses.
- Combining that result with external priors constrains the low-mass IMF slope and cutoff.

**Use when:**
- Citing quasar-microlensing constraints on the low-mass IMF of lens galaxies.

**Do not use for:**
- The IMF of an individual lens galaxy or an IMF constraint independent of external priors.
