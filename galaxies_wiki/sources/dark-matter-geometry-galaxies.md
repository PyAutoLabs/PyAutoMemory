# Sources: galaxy-scale dark-matter geometry

Papers on the spatial / kinematic alignment of satellite galaxies with
their host halos and central galaxies — geometric probes of dark-matter
halo shapes at galaxy scales. Distinct from cluster-lensing DM-shape
work in `../lensing_wiki/`.
