# Sources: SMBH recoil and core scouring

Papers on the gravitational-wave recoil of merged SMBHs and the
dynamical scouring of nuclear stellar populations during binary
inspiral. Direct relevance to PyAutoLens: cored galaxies appear as
shallow inner-light gradients that interact with mass-profile fits.

## Thomas 2013 — dynamical footprint of core scouring

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Thomas2014`
**Reference:** The dynamical fingerprint of core scouring in massive elliptical galaxies; arXiv:1311.3783; doi:10.1088/0004-637x/782/1/39; ApJ
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[etg-radial-gradients]]

**Supports:**
- Connects depleted stellar cores in massive ellipticals to SMBH-binary scouring.
- Uses stellar dynamics to identify signatures expected from core scouring.
- Provides a reference for interpreting galaxy cores as dynamical SMBH-binary remnants.

**Use when:**
- Citing the dynamical footprint of core scouring in massive elliptical galaxies.

**Do not use for:**
- PTA gravitational-wave-background measurements or black-hole seed models.


## Kormendy & Bender 2013 — cores in ellipticals

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Kormendy2013a`
**Reference:** The L $\alpha$ $\sigma$8 correlation for elliptical galaxies with cores: Relation with black hole mass; doi:10.1088/2041-8205/769/1/l5; ApJL
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[etg-structure]]

**Supports:**
- Relates core elliptical-galaxy properties to black-hole mass.
- Uses core-galaxy structural correlations as SMBH-host evidence.
- Provides context for depleted cores in the most massive ellipticals.

**Use when:**
- Citing core elliptical structural correlations with SMBH mass.

**Do not use for:**
- Direct SMBH mass measurement in a single galaxy unless the relation is the target.
