# NANOGrav

## What it is

The North American Nanohertz Observatory for Gravitational Waves
(NANOGrav). A pulsar-timing-array collaboration monitoring ~70+
millisecond pulsars with Arecibo (until 2020), Green Bank, and the VLA
to detect the nanohertz stochastic GW background.

## Key facts

- 15-year dataset (2023): claimed first significant Hellings–Downs
  correlation, interpreted as evidence for an SMBHB-sourced SGWB.
- Sister consortia: EPTA (European), PPTA (Parkes), IPTA (combined).
- Constrains: SGWB amplitude (and thus high-mass-end SMBH mass
  function), individual continuous-wave SMBHB candidates, cosmic
  strings / inflationary backgrounds.
