# RABBITS

## What it is

The RABBITS simulation suite (Liao et al.) — high-resolution
hydrodynamic simulations of galaxy mergers tracking SMBH binary
coalescence with explicit hardening physics. Designed to test whether
nuclear star formation refills the loss cone enough to drive binaries
through the "final parsec".

## Key facts

- Headline result (paper II): nuclear star formation drives SMBH
  binaries to coalescence within a few hundred Myr in most simulated
  mergers, resolving the final-parsec problem in the high-mass regime.
- Sister suite: KETJU ([[ketju]]) takes a complementary approach with
  explicit regularised gravitational dynamics.
