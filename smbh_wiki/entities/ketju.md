# KETJU

## What it is

A simulation framework (Mannerkoski / Rantala et al.) that couples
regularised stellar dynamics around SMBH binaries to large-scale
hydrodynamic galaxy simulations. Used to track SMBH-binary scouring
and final-parsec evolution self-consistently with cosmological
context.

## Key facts

- Resolves the SMBHB hardening phase directly rather than via
  sub-grid prescription.
- Predicts core sizes and mass deficits in massive ETGs consistent
  with observations.
- Sister suite: [[rabbits]] (hydro-focused).
