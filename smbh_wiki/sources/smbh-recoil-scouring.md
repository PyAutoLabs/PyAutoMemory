# Sources: SMBH recoil and core scouring

Papers on the gravitational-wave recoil of merged SMBHs and the
dynamical scouring of nuclear stellar populations during binary
inspiral. Direct relevance to PyAutoLens: cored galaxies appear as
shallow inner-light gradients that interact with mass-profile fits.

## Thomas 2013 — dynamical footprint of core scouring

**File:** `SMBHs/Thomas2013DynamicalFootprintCoreScouring.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[etg-radial-gradients]]
**Summary (stub):** Identifies the kinematic and stellar-population
signature of binary-driven core scouring in BCGs and massive ellipticals.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Kormendy & Bender 2013 — cores in ellipticals

**File:** `SMBHs/Kormendy2013CoresEll.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[etg-structure]]
**Summary (stub):** Catalogues "core" vs "extra-light" ETGs, attributing
the cores to dry-merger / SMBH-binary scouring.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Hlavacek-Larrondo 2021 — SMBHs in BCGs

**File:** `SMBHs/Hlavacak2021SMBHsinBCGClusters.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[smbh-host-scaling]]
**Summary (stub):** Demographics of SMBHs in brightest cluster
galaxies, scaling with cluster mass and central radio AGN power.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Hlavacek-Larrondo — UMBH

**File:** `SMBHs/HlavacekUMBH.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[smbh-mass-measurement]]
**Summary (stub):** Ultra-massive black holes in cluster BCGs, mass
estimates and host correlations.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Mahler 2022 — SMBHs in clusters

**File:** `SMBHs/Maheler2022SMBHInClusters.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-scouring]], [[smbh-host-scaling]]
**Summary (stub):** Constraints on SMBH masses in cluster BCGs from
strong-lensing-aided dynamical modelling.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Liao 2024 — RABBITS II

**File:** `SMBHs/Liao2024RABBITS2.pdf`
**arXiv:** 2311.01499
**Concepts:** [[smbh-binaries]], [[smbh-scouring]]
**Summary (stub):** Second RABBITS simulation paper showing that nuclear
star formation drives SMBH binaries to coalescence, with implications
for the binary lifetime distribution feeding the nanohertz SGWB.
(stub — verify against PDF)
