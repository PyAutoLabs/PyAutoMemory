# Sources: SMBH host-galaxy scaling relations

Papers on the empirical correlations between SMBH mass and host-galaxy
properties: M–σ, M–M_bulge, M–M_halo, and their evolution. Each entry is
filename-inferred and unread.

## Ferrarese & Merritt 2000 — M–σ relation

**File:** `SMBHs/Ferrarese2000VeloDisp.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-host-scaling]]
**Summary (stub):** One of the two co-discovery papers establishing the
tight correlation between SMBH mass and host-galaxy bulge velocity
dispersion (the M–σ relation).
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Gebhardt 2000 — M–σ relation

**File:** `SMBHs/Gebhardt2000SMBHVelDisp.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-host-scaling]]
**Summary (stub):** Companion discovery paper to Ferrarese & Merritt 2000
of the M–σ relation, using a complementary galaxy sample.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Graham 2001 — SMBH–Sérsic-index correlation

**File:** `SMBHs/Grapham2001SMBHSersic.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-host-scaling]], [[sersic-profile]]
**Summary (stub):** Correlation between SMBH mass and the Sérsic index
of the host spheroid, suggesting a structural rather than purely
kinematic link.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Graham 2016 — galactic bulges and SMBHs

**File:** `SMBHs/Grapham2016GalactricBulbges.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-host-scaling]], [[bulges-vs-pseudobulges]]
**Summary (stub):** Updated correlations between SMBH masses and
host-bulge properties, separating classical bulges from pseudo-bulges.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Kormendy & Ho 2013 — coevolution review

**File:** `SMBHs/Kormandy2013CoEvoOrNot.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-host-scaling]]
**Summary (stub):** Influential ARA&A-style review arguing that SMBHs
coevolve with classical bulges and ellipticals, but not with discs or
pseudo-bulges — refines the M–M_bulge relation accordingly.
(stub — verify against PDF)
