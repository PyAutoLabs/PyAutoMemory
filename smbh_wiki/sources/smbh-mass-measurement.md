# Sources: SMBH mass measurement

Papers on the techniques used to weigh supermassive black holes:
spatially-resolved stellar / gas dynamics, broad-line reverberation
mapping, ALMA molecular-gas dynamics, and the EHT.

## Kormendy & Richstone 1995 — search inward

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Kormendy1995`
**Reference:** Inward Bound---The Search For Supermassive Black Holes In Galactic Nuclei; Annual Review of Astronomy and Astrophysics
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]

**Supports:**
- Reviews the observational search for supermassive black holes in galactic nuclei.
- Summarizes early stellar/gas dynamical evidence for nuclear black holes.
- Provides historical context for direct SMBH mass-measurement methods.

**Use when:**
- Citing the early review/history of direct SMBH searches in galaxy nuclei.

**Do not use for:**
- Modern ALMA molecular-gas measurements or post-2010 scaling-relation calibrations.

## Shen 2013 — quasar BH masses

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Shen2013`
**Reference:** The mass of quasars; arXiv:1302.2643; Bulletin of the Astronomical Society of India
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]

**Supports:**
- Reviews what quasar black-hole mass estimates measure and the limits of those estimates.
- Provides context for broad-line/single-epoch quasar SMBH mass inference.
- Useful for caveats on quasar mass estimates rather than a new local dynamical measurement.

**Use when:**
- Citing conceptual limitations or interpretation of quasar SMBH mass estimates.

## Davis 2017 — molecular gas dynamics

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Davis2017a`
**Reference:** WISDOM Project - II. Molecular gas measurement of the supermassive black hole mass in NGC 4697; arXiv:1703.05248; doi:10.1093/mnras/stw3217; MNRAS
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]

**Supports:**
- Measures the SMBH mass in NGC 4697 using molecular-gas dynamics.
- Uses ALMA/WISDOM-style molecular gas observations as a direct dynamical mass tracer.
- Provides an early-type-galaxy example of CO/gas-dynamical SMBH mass measurement.

**Use when:**
- Citing molecular-gas dynamical SMBH mass measurement with ALMA/WISDOM.

**Do not use for:**
- Quasar virial mass estimates or population-level mass functions.

## Thater 2019 — ALMA robustness

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Thater2019`
**Reference:** Testing the robustness of black hole mass measurements with ALMA and MUSE; arXiv:1911.11491; doi:10.1017/s1743921319008445; Proceedings of the International Astronomical Union
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]

**Supports:**
- Tests robustness of black-hole mass measurements that combine ALMA and MUSE constraints.
- Addresses modelling/systematic checks for gas- and stellar-dynamical SMBH inference.
- Useful as a methodological reference for validating SMBH mass measurements.

**Use when:**
- Citing robustness/systematics tests in ALMA/MUSE SMBH mass modelling.

**Do not use for:**
- The first detection of an SMBH in a specific galaxy.

## Ding 2019 — SMBH-galaxy correlation high-z

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Ding2019`
**Reference:** The Mass Relations between Supermassive Black Holes and Their Host Galaxies at 1 \textless z \textless 2 with HST -WFC3; arXiv:1910.11875; doi:10.3847/1538-4357/ab5b90; The Astrophysical Journal
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]], [[smbh-host-scaling]]

**Supports:**
- Measures SMBH-host mass relations at 1 < z < 2 with HST/WFC3 data.
- Extends SMBH-host correlation studies beyond the local Universe.
- Provides a high-redshift comparison point for black-hole/host-galaxy coevolution.

**Use when:**
- Citing high-redshift SMBH-host mass relations from HST imaging.

**Do not use for:**
- Local M–σ calibration or direct ALMA gas-dynamical masses.
