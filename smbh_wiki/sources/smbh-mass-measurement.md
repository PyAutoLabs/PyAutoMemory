# Sources: SMBH mass measurement

Papers on the techniques used to weigh supermassive black holes:
spatially-resolved stellar / gas dynamics, broad-line reverberation
mapping, ALMA molecular-gas dynamics, and the EHT.

## Kormendy & Richstone 1995 — search inward

**File:** `SMBHs/Kormendy1995SearchInward.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]
**Summary (stub):** Early review of stellar-dynamical SMBH detections
in nearby galactic nuclei, establishing the demographics of SMBHs.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## Peterson 2004 — broad-line region

**File:** `SMBHs/Peterson2004BroadLineRegion.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]
**Summary (stub):** Definitive review of reverberation-mapping
methodology for measuring AGN SMBH masses via the BLR.
(stub — verify against PDF)

## McClure 2014 — quasar BH masses

**File:** `SMBHs/Mcclure2014QuasarBHMasses.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[smbh-mass-measurement]]

(stub — verify against PDF)
