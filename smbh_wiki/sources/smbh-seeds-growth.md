# Sources: SMBH seeds, growth, and the high-z frontier

Papers on the seeding (light Pop III remnants vs heavy direct-collapse),
super-Eddington accretion, and the JWST-era population of "overmassive"
SMBHs at z > 4.

from quasar luminosity functions and host properties.
