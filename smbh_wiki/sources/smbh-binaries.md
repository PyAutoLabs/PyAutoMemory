# Sources: SMBH binaries

Papers on the formation, hardening, observational signatures, and
final-coalescence physics of supermassive black hole binaries (SMBHBs).

## Shen 2013 — BBHs via BLR time variations

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Version2018`
**Reference:** Constraining sub-parsec binary supermassive black holes in quasars with multi-epoch spectroscopy. I. the general quasar population; arXiv:1306.4330; doi:10.1088/0004-637x/775/1/49; Astrophysical Journal
**Concepts:** [[smbh-binaries]], [[dual-agn]]

**Supports:**
- Uses multi-epoch quasar spectroscopy to search for sub-parsec SMBH-binary candidates via broad-line velocity changes.
- Frames broad-line-region velocity offsets and accelerations as a binary-SMBH selection diagnostic.
- Applies the method to the general quasar population rather than to a single confirmed binary.

**Use when:**
- Citing spectroscopic broad-line variability searches for sub-parsec SMBH binaries in quasars.

**Do not use for:**
- Confirmed binary demographics or gravitational-wave-background amplitudes.


## Liao 2024 — RABBITS II (cross-listed)

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Liao2024`
**Reference:** RABBITS – II. The impact of AGN feedback on coalescing supermassive black holes in disc and elliptical galaxy mergers; arXiv:2311.01493; doi:10.1093/mnras/stae1123; MNRAS
**Concepts:** [[smbh-binaries]], [[final-parsec-problem]]

**Supports:**
- Studies coalescing SMBHs in disc and elliptical galaxy merger simulations.
- Tests how AGN feedback changes the merger remnant and SMBH coalescence environment.
- Provides a RABBITS-series simulation reference for binary-SMBH evolution in galaxy mergers.

**Use when:**
- Citing simulated SMBH coalescence in galaxy mergers with AGN-feedback context.

**Do not use for:**
- Observational PTA constraints or empirical SMBH-host scaling relations.
